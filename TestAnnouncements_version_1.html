Testing Announcement

PK recently launched a test on mobile PIPs

Stacked YMAN

On Monday, PK launched a mobile test showing YMAN product recommendations vertically stacked, instead of on a horizontal scroll. We think this will improve user interaction with the YMAN recommendations, and will improve product discovery.

We expect users to find more relevant products with less friction, which will ultimately drive improved conversion and RPV.

Test Duration: 10/14-10/25

(~9 days)

Control

Horizontal YMAN

Variant

Vertical YMAN

Hypothesis

We believe showing YMAN recommendations in a stacked module on Mobile PIPs will improve user’s ability to engage with YMAN and facilitate product discovery. We believe this will ultimately improve purchase conversion and lift RPV.

Audience

Visitors on Mobile PIP with YMAN present

  • 50% to Control: Horizontal scrolling YMAN
  • 50% to Test: Vertically stacked YMAN
